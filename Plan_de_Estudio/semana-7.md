### 🎵 Música — Tonalidad Fa Mayor

**Fa Mayor: SI BEMOL (Bb).**

| Tonalidad    | Notas                | Alteración    |
| ------------ | -------------------- | ------------- |
| Do Mayor     | C D E F G A B C      | ♮ ninguna     |
| Sol Mayor    | G A B C D E F# G     | 1 ♯ (Fa#)     |
| **Fa Mayor** | **F G A Bb C D E F** | **1 ♭ (Sib)** |

**Armadura:** un bemol en la línea del Si.

**Patrón de semitonos:** F→G (T), G→A (T), A→Bb (S), Bb→C (T), C→D (T), D→E (T), E→F (S).
El semitono está entre A-Bb (3-4) y E-F (7-8).

**Práctica:**

1. Escribí F G A Bb C D E F. Rodéa el Sib.
2. MusicTheory.net: activá "Key Signature: F Major".
3. Identificá las 8 notas de Fa M.

## 📅 Día 2 — Viernes 31/7 (40 min EN + 20 min música)

### 🇬🇧 Inglés — "Going to" Futuro

**¿Cuándo se usa?**

**"Going to"** expresa planes o intenciones FUTURAS (ya decidiste hacerlo).
También predicciones basadas en evidencia.

|           | Afirmativa             | Negativa                  | Pregunta                |
| --------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| I         | I'm **going to study** | I'm not going to study    | Am I going to study?    |
| You       | You're going to play   | You aren't going to play  | Are you going to play?  |
| He/She/It | He's going to travel   | He isn't going to travel  | Is he going to travel?  |
| We        | We're going to eat     | We aren't going to eat    | Are we going to eat?    |
| They      | They're going to swim  | They aren't going to swim | Are they going to swim? |

**Estructura:** Sujeto + BE (am/are/is) + GOING TO + VERBO BASE

**⚠️ Error común #1:** "I going to study" (NO — falta el BE: "I'm going to study").
**⚠️ Error común #2:** "I'm gonna study" (informal escrito — bien para hablar, pero escribí "going to").

**📖 Reading A2 (15 min):**

1. Buscá "My next holiday" o "Plans for next weekend" en British Council.
2. Marcá TODAS las frases con "going to".
3. 5 palabras nuevas a Anki.

**📐 Grammar (15 min):**

**🎧 Listening A1 (10 min):**
