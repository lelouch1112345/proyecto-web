# AGENTS.md — proyecto-web

## What this repo is

A **science-backed 3-language + music study plan** for achieving fluency in English, Spanish, and Japanese while developing musical literacy. This is NOT a traditional codebase — it's a structured learning system with daily tasks, progress tracking, and evidence-based methodologies.

## How to use this repo

### Daily study workflow

1. **Check current week**: Look at `Plan_de_Estudio/mes-X-completo.md` for the month
2. **Find today's plan**: Each day has specific English + music tasks (Japanese starts week 2)
3. **Follow the 4-step cycle**:
   - Reading: 1x, then close and summarize aloud (retrieval practice)
   - Speaking: Self-record introductions, minimal pairs practice
   - Listening: Shadowing with exact intonation
   - Anki: 5-10 new words per day

### Key constraints to respect

- **100% English days**: No Japanese during English-focused weeks
- **40 min consistency**: Daily over 3h intensity
- **7-8h sleep**: Critical for consolidation

### Emergency recovery

If you miss days: `Plan_de_Estudio/emergencia.md` — follow the protocol immediately

## Study methodology (what makes this unique)

### Science-backed techniques

- **FSRS-5**: Advanced spaced repetition algorithm
- **Interleaving**: Mix different skills within sessions
- **Retrieval practice**: Active recall over passive reading
- **Minimal pairs**: Focused pronunciation drills (ship vs sheep)
- **Pre-sleep review**: Consolidate learning before bed

### Language progression

- **Week 1**: English + Music only (build foundation)
- **Week 2+**: Add Japanese (separate attention blocks)
- **Concurrent**: English + Spanish + Japanese across months
